How to answer 'Sell Me This Pen' like The Wolf Of Wall Street

19 Nov, 2024. Tuesday

Don't do a salesman pitch. Don't try to sell it like a normal retail product.

You can try this tricky demand and supply kind of sale like this as they did in the movie Wolf Of Wall Street. You can Watch the scene here.Though this scene explains Supply and Demand, It's not that specific.

So, what's the best way to sell a pen?

Read this little conversation below.

Interviewer : Sell me 'This Pen'.

You : Okay. A quick question for you. Are you someone who just pick any random pen or the pen which is right for you?

Interviewer : Of course, the one which is right for me.

You : Great! Here in our company we design pen according to your standards. So the pen fits you. 'You' alone. Because each and every part of the pen is designed with 'You' in mind. Either you fit to the pen or the pen fits 'You'. Sounds fair enough?

Now what if the interviewer says 'I'll pick a random pen?'
