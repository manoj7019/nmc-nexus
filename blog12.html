How to negotiate the salary to higher range?

3 Dec, 2024. Tuesday

(**Note : You must have enough skills required for that job role. That's no excuse.)

Make sure you already know the salary range they've mentioned in the job description they've posted.

When the interviewer asks - What's your salary expectation? Give the strategic answer by following this 4 step pattern.

#1 Show them you're aware of the salary range they're offering.
Eg: "As I noticed in Job Description, the salary range aligns well with my expectations."

#2 Let them know you're flexible with that salary range and open for negotiation based on job role and benefits.
Eg: "I'm flexible within that range and would be happy to discuss based on roles and responsibilities."

#3 Justify how valuable you are with your skills, certifications, experience(if any) and place yourself high.
Eg: "Through my [specific skills, experience and certifications], I believe I'll bring significant value to this role in all the ways humanly possible. So I think I deserve higher end of the package range."

#4 Redirect focus to Company's contribution
Eg: "Though salary plays a major role, My sheer focus any day is excellence and contribution to the team and company's growth. I'll work for it as hard as humanly possible."

Here's how it looks when combined :

"As I noticed in Job Description, the salary range aligns well with my expectations. I'm flexible within that range and would be happy to discuss based on roles and responsibilities. Through my [specific skills, experience and certifications], I believe I'll bring significant value to this role in all the ways humanly possible. So I think I deserve higher end of the package range. Though salary plays a major role, My sheer focus any day is excellence and contribution to the team and company's growth. I'll work for it as hard as humanly possible."
