What's the fastest way to learn any skill?

28 Nov, 2024. Thursday

Spend time doing it.
That's it. You can stop reading further right now.

When I started to learn coding, I spent endless hours watching 12-hour-long tutorials. I hopped from one course to another every week, but still, I couldn't write a single program when I sat in front of a blank screen.

One day, something clicked into my mind. I sketched something on my board to impress my friend. I didn't know how to build it, but I did what any sane person can do to learn that - I just googled it.

I learned that specific part of the program and built it. I just sat, spent enough time, and did it. Even when I wanted to learn social media marketing, I watched lot of videos, but they didn't help. I just grabbed a book from Gary Vaynerchuk. One of the experts in the field, just followed step by step what he said, and applied it.

There's a quote from one of the greatest writers ever lived: "There is nothing to writing. All you do is sit down at a typewriter and bleed." - Ernst Hemmingway.

The quote applies to any skill. As you know, writing is one of the hardest skill on the planet.
