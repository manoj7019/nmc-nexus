Should I Work in a Startup or in a Big Company?

23 Nov, 2024. Saturday

It's a hard choice to make and it depends completely on your priorities. Both have their own pros and cons. But you can have a look at the comparison below and make a choice.

Topic Startup (Small New Company) MNC (Big Famous Company)
Growing in your job You can get promoted fast, but there might not be many new positions after a while. You know exactly how to climb the ladder, but it takes time because so many people are in line.
Learning new skills You do lots of different things, so you learn quickly, but no fancy training programs. You get proper training, but you might only learn one specific thing.
Work environment It’s fun and exciting, but you might have to work long hours to meet deadlines. Everything is organized, but the rules can feel strict or boring.
Job safety Great if the company succeeds, but risky if it doesn’t. Your job is safe most of the time, but big companies can also have layoffs.
Pay (money) You might earn less at first, but you could get shares if the company becomes super successful. You earn a steady, good salary, but no shares in the company.
Getting noticed It’s easy to stand out in a small team, but you’ll feel pressure to always perform well. There are awards for great work, but it’s hard to stand out in such a big team.
Tools and resources You have to get creative because there might not be a lot of fancy tools. You’ll have access to the latest and greatest tools and gadgets.
How fast you learn You’ll learn really fast because you’ll try so many different things, but it might feel overwhelming. You’ll learn step-by-step, which is easier, but it might feel slower.
