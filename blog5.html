The only course you need to learn English Speaking

15 Nov, 2024. Friday

To be frank, No Course will help you learn English. But your desire to learn and speak.

Still there are many effortless ways to speed up your learning.

1. Binge-watch English Movies/Series with Captions.
Watching movies will enhance your English fluency. You will learn the slang and new phrases. Movies can improve your vocabulary and listening skills.

2. Start Listening to English Music.
Listen to Adele, Alan Walker, Marshmallow, Ed Sheeran, Taylor Swift, John Legend’s music. Because their songs are easy to understand and perfect for English learning.

3. Start Reading Books and Novels.

4. Use Duolingo for 10 mins daily.
Learning in Duolingo is fun and addictive! Duolingo breaks down learning to bite-sized lessons and easy to consume. Start. You’ll never stop using it.

5. Write something daily (Journal).
Writing will force you to get things out of your mind, learn new words, express your feelings. Improve your writing by using synonyms by searching on Google.
