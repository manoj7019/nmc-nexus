Hey, Tell Me About Yourself.

15 Nov, 2024. Friday

Seems to be a simple question. But sometimes we make it more complicated.

You know why interviewers ask this question?

1. It helps to ease the interview.
2. It builds the interview in terms of what questions to ask next.
3. It shows your communication skills.

Now you know ‘WHY?’. We’ll tell you 'HOW?'

(** This is your first impression. You don’t want to ruin it.)

According to Lily Zhang (Former Manager of Graduate Student Professional Development, MIT Media Lab), Here’s what you want to do (in 3 steps)

1. Present
Talk about
- Your current role.
- How long you have been working here.
- What’s your accomplishment (in metrics)
Example :
I’m working as a Digital marketer for ‘X’ company from ‘Y’ months. I increased their leads by ‘Z’%.

2. Past
Tell them
- Why you’re making this career change.
- How it will benefit the company and you.
Example :
I want to outwork my potential by exploring and contributing more in this field and I think your company is the best place to start.

Here’s how it looks when combined :

I’m Manoj, I’m working as a Digital marketer for NMC India from 6 months. I increased their leads by 15%.

Actually I got certified as a Digital Marketer from Google’s online course. Those skills helped me to land my job in NMC India and work with them.

And I want to outwork my potential by exploring and contributing more in this field and I think your company is the best place to start.

You can use this idea and tweak it to match your profession,skills and experience and increase your chances of creating a stunning first impression.
