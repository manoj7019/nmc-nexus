Harsh realities of IT Industry you must know

15 Nov, 2024. Friday

1. The percentage of people working in IT Industry without a degree is 0.1%.
If anybody tells “You don’t need a college degree”, Just laugh at their face.

2. Always expect to deal with unknown things daily.
Don’t expect that a degree, bootcamp or an internship is sufficient enough to make your job life easy.

3. Don’t expect to make a ton of money if you lack passion for the field or if you refuse to grow your skills.
When you commit to CS, you commit to lifelong learning.

4. The amount of effort is equally distributed amongst the team.
If you have skills, you doesn’t have to work as hard as the ones with worse skills.

5. Communication skills are equally important as your technical skills.
If you think you can work in isolation ignoring everyone, You are wrong!

6. For a newbie, Remote jobs will not help to grow fast unless you’re consistently proactive.
You'll miss many opportunities if you're not in the office.

7. Most obvious one.
Your performance in an interview matters more than the skills in you resume.

Don’t panic. This is how it works. Be ready to face it.
